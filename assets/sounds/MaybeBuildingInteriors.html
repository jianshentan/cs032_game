Dragan Espenschied, founding member of the folk duo Bodenständig 2000, composes music for sound chips found in vintage computers, like theCommodore VIC20 or the Atari ST.

Creating new tunes takes him forever, but when it happens you can expect something truly poetic, and a level of attention to detail that seems impossible with these simple instruments.

Upitup is releasing his series "Education of the Noobz"! Expect a new album every 4 to 6 years, and expect your heart to be warmed Quad Core.
